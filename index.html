About Us

Our mission is to encourage citizens to report waste, keep cities clean, and reward them for contributing to a better environment.

  • ✅ Report waste in your area
  • ✅ Help AI detect issues
  • ✅ Earn rewards for participation

The Authority aims to organize, follow up, monitor, evaluate and develop everything related to integrated waste management activities, attract and encourage investments in the field of integrated waste management activities to ensure the achievement of sustainable development, and follow up on the implementation of the necessary plans to organize waste management in cooperation with state institutions, local administrations, the private sector, civil society organizations and international organizations. The Authority also aims to support relations between the Arab Republic of Egypt and countries and international and regional organizations in the field of waste management.
